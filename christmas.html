How to Play 2048 Cupcakes Christmas Game

Use your arrow keys (also W, A, S, and D keys alternatively) or swipe with your fingers to move the 2048 Cupcakes Christmas tiles. For example, if you joined 2 tiles with the number 2 Christmas Cupcakes, they will become 4 Christmas Cupcakes, 2 Christmas Cupcakes tiles with 4 Christmas Cupcakes will become 8 Christmas Cupcakes, and so on. The point of the game is to get the 2048 Christmas Cupcakes tile!
