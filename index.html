How to Play 2048 Cupcakes

Use your arrow keys (also W, A, S, and D keys alternatively) or swipe with your fingers to move the 2048 Cupcakes tiles. For example, if you joined 2 tiles with the number 2 Cupcakes, they will become 4 Cupcakes, 2 Cupcakes tiles with 4 Cupcakes will become 8 Cupcakes, and so on. The point of the game is to get the 2048 Cupcakes tile!
